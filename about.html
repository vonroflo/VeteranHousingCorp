About Veteran Housing Corp

A 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to ending veteran homelessness in Marion County, Florida — one veteran, one home, one future at a time.

Our Mission

Our prime directive is to provide safe, decent, and affordable housing to veterans — because housing is not a privilege. It’s the foundation of recovery.

The Problem

Veteran homelessness is a national disgrace and a local crisis. Many of those who served their country come home to find no place to call their own — and the path back to stability is steeper, lonelier, and harder than it ever should be.

On any given night in Florida, over 2,000 veterans are without a home.

Marion County, Florida — and the surrounding communities we serve — are not exempt. Behind every statistic is a person: a sailor, a soldier, an airman, a marine. Each deserves better. Each deserves a door of their own.

Our Approach

A collaborative, evidence-based model. We partner directly with the VA, Marion County, and trusted local nonprofits to wrap each veteran in the support they need.

Housing First

Stability begins with shelter. We house veterans without preconditions, then build everything else on that foundation.

Wraparound Support

Case management, mental health resources, employment coaching, and benefits navigation — coordinated alongside VA partnership services.

Community Integration

Connecting veterans to the neighborhoods, networks, and opportunities that turn a roof into a home and a home into a future.
